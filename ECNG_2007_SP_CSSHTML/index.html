Welcome Message

Welcome to the PCR(Polymerase Chain Reaction) Test Appointment System. As quarantine protocols such as travel and business restictions are being lifted, a test is required to engage in these activities. Within Trinidad and Tobago, public health centers operate on a "walk in" basis. This website implements fast and simple scheduling of PCR appointments based on your purpose for obtaining a test. This implementation also limits the number of persons entering the facility which helps with adherence to National health regulations and protocols.
